Visualization





See How Vaccinations Are Going in Your County and State



The above visualization shows the total number of people in the United States who are fully vaccinated area-wise. The dull color orange represents the areas where less than 30% of the people are fully vaccinated. The dark green color represents the areas where more than 70% of the people are fully vaccinated. In addition, the Colors used in this plot are correctly contrasted and give a good understanding of how many more areas need to be fully vaccinated and how many are fully vaccinated and safe. I think this visualization would help Government to organize more vaccination awareness camps in the less vaccinated areas.
